About Us

Qwetu is home to more than 600 students ( both men and women). Apart from providing a serene environment for studying and good night's sleep, students also get a chance to enjoy cultural diversity. The major facilities available to all our residents are 1 or 2 or 3 bedroom hostels, constant water supply, a free membership gym, wifi ( optional at at different price rates), a backup generator, and security around the clock among others.
