About Hostel

Qwetu is home to more than 600 students. Apart from providing a serene environment for studying and good night's sleep, students also get a chance to enjoy cultural diversity. The major facilities available to all our residents are 1/2/3 bedroom hostels, constant water supply, a free membership gym, wifi(optional), a backup generator , and security around the clock among others.
